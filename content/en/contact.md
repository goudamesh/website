## Privacy & Data

**Website:** This website is completely tracker-free. We do not use cookies and do not collect any data about our visitors. Your visit is therefore anonymous.

**The Network:** GoudaMesh monitors public traffic on the default channel (LongFast). The statistics and messages we gather from this are used exclusively to map the coverage and activity of the network.

Important to know:

*   **Public Domain:** The LongFast channel is public by nature; all information sent over it can be intercepted by anyone with an antenna.
*   **Personal Responsibility:** Do you want private communication? Please use private channels (with your own encryption keys) or Direct Messages. This data is encrypted and cannot be read by us or others.
*   **Location:** You are in full control of sharing your GPS location via your node settings.
